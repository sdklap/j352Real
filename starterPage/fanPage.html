RuPaul's Drag Race

Drag queens from around the country come to compete on RuPaul's Drag Race for a cash prize of $100,000. Each week, the queens compete in a challenge and present an outfit on a runway. They are judged by a panel of judges including RuPaul Charles, Michelle Visage, Ross Matthews, and a guest judge. Common challenges include a RuSical, girl group, and unconventional materials challenge. The franchise has expanded so much, that they now have shows in the UK, Australia, Canada, Thailand, Italy, Spain, Holland, and Chile.
